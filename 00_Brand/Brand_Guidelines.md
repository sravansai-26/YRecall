# YRecall Brand Guidelines

Version: 1.0

Last Updated: July 2026

---

# Brand Overview

YRecall is an AI-powered Life Operating System designed to help people capture, understand, organize, connect, and recall everything that matters in their lives.

Unlike traditional note-taking applications, productivity tools, or AI chatbots, YRecall acts as a continuous intelligence layer that transforms scattered information into connected knowledge.

The brand represents clarity, trust, intelligence, simplicity, and calmness.

---

# Brand Vision

To become the world's most trusted AI Life Operating System that helps people remember less, understand more, and live better.

---

# Brand Mission

Build an intelligent personal companion that continuously understands a person's life, automatically organizes information, and provides meaningful insights whenever needed.

---

# Brand Positioning

YRecall is not:

* A notes application
* A task manager
* A file storage platform
* A traditional AI chatbot

YRecall is:

* A Life Operating System
* A Personal Knowledge Platform
* An AI Memory Companion
* A Context Intelligence Engine

---

# Brand Personality

YRecall should always feel:

* Calm
* Helpful
* Intelligent
* Trustworthy
* Friendly
* Human
* Minimal
* Professional
* Private
* Reliable

Never feel:

* Loud
* Flashy
* Overwhelming
* Futuristic for the sake of appearance
* Gimmicky
* Robotic
* Aggressive

---

# Core Brand Values

## Trust

User data belongs to the user.

Transparency is mandatory.

Privacy comes before personalization.

---

## Simplicity

Complex technology should feel effortless.

The interface should remove cognitive load instead of adding it.

---

## Intelligence

AI should understand context, not just answer questions.

The product should continuously learn while remaining explainable.

---

## Continuity

Every memory contributes to a lifelong knowledge network.

Nothing meaningful should become isolated.

---

## Human-Centered Design

Technology exists to help people.

Every feature should reduce friction and improve everyday life.

---

Capture once.

Recall instantly.

# Visual Identity

Design Language

* Material Design 3 inspired
* Apple Human Interface inspired
* Minimal
* Rounded
* Spacious
* Clean
* Calm
* Elegant

Avoid

* Heavy Glassmorphism
* Neomorphism
* Brutalism
* Cyberpunk aesthetics
* Dark cinematic visuals
* Excessive gradients

---

# Color Philosophy

Primary

Deep Blue

Represents intelligence and trust.

Secondary

Teal

Represents clarity and connection.

Accent

Warm Orange

Represents AI suggestions and important actions.

Background

Warm White

Surface

Soft Neutral Gray

Colors should support readability and calmness rather than attract unnecessary attention.

---

# Typography

Use one modern sans-serif family consistently.

Typography should prioritize readability over decoration.

Hierarchy

* Display
* Headline
* Title
* Body
* Caption
* Label

---

# Iconography

Icons should be:

* Rounded
* Minimal
* Consistent
* Outline-first
* Filled only for active states

Do not mix multiple icon styles.

---

# Motion Principles

Animations should communicate state changes.

Motion should feel:

* Smooth
* Natural
* Purposeful
* Subtle

Avoid decorative animations that distract from content.

---

# Illustration Style

Illustrations should:

* Be simple
* Be friendly
* Explain concepts
* Use minimal colors
* Support onboarding and empty states

Avoid cartoonish or exaggerated artwork.

---

# Tone of Voice

The product should communicate like a thoughtful companion.

Examples

Instead of

"No Results Found"

Use

"We couldn't find anything yet. Try searching differently or capture something new."

Instead of

"Error"

Use

"Something interrupted the request. Your data is safe. Please try again."

---

# Logo Principles

The logo should be:

* Simple
* Memorable
* Scalable
* Recognizable at small sizes
* Suitable for app icons and favicons

Avoid unnecessary detail.

# Long-Term Brand Vision

YRecall is intended to become the flagship product of the LYFSpot ecosystem.

As the platform evolves, future LYFSpot products will connect through YRecall's knowledge layer, allowing users to experience a unified, intelligent digital ecosystem rather than isolated applications.

The goal is not simply to build software.

The goal is to build a lifelong AI companion that grows alongside every user.
