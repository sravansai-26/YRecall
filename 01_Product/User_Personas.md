# Purpose

This document defines the primary users of YRecall.

Every feature, workflow, design decision, engineering priority, marketing strategy, and business decision should be evaluated against these personas.

The objective is not to build for everyone.

The objective is to solve meaningful problems exceptionally well for clearly defined users.

---

# Primary User Groups

YRecall primarily serves people who work with information, ideas, decisions, relationships, and long-term knowledge.

The first version of YRecall focuses on six primary personas.

* Strong privacy controls.
* Fast performance.
* Offline capability.
* Reliable synchronization.
* Accessibility.

---

# Personas We Are NOT Building For (Initially)

To maintain focus during early development, YRecall is **not** primarily designed for:

* Social media users seeking entertainment.
* Children under 13.
* Gaming communities.
* Creative design software workflows.
* Large enterprise knowledge management systems.
* Industry-specific ERP or CRM replacements.

These audiences may be supported in the future but are outside the scope of Version 1.

---

# Product Prioritization Framework

When evaluating new features, ask:

1. Which persona benefits most?
2. Which problem does it solve?
3. Does it reduce cognitive load?
4. Does it strengthen the user's personal knowledge network?
5. Will it improve daily engagement?
6. Is it aligned with YRecall's vision?

If the answer to most of these questions is "no," the feature should be reconsidered or deferred.

---

# Conclusion

YRecall is designed for people whose lives revolve around knowledge, ideas, relationships, and decisions.

Rather than trying to satisfy every possible user, the product focuses on delivering exceptional value to users who benefit most from intelligent memory, contextual understanding, and AI-powered personal organization.

As the platform matures, additional personas and industries will be supported while maintaining the same core philosophy:

**Help people remember less, understand more, and live with greater clarity.**
