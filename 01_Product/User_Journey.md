# Purpose

This document defines the complete lifecycle of a YRecall user.

It describes every major interaction—from discovering YRecall for the first time to becoming a long-term, highly engaged user.

The purpose of this document is to help Product, Design, Engineering, AI, Marketing, and Customer Success teams understand:

* How users enter the ecosystem.
* What motivates them.
* What actions they take.
* What problems they encounter.
* How YRecall delivers value at every stage.
* How users become loyal advocates.

Every product improvement should strengthen one or more stages of this journey.

# Potential Friction Points

Users may abandon the journey if:

* Onboarding is too long.
* Permissions feel intrusive.
* AI makes obvious mistakes.
* Search is unreliable.
* Capture feels slow.
* Synchronization fails.
* Privacy is unclear.
* The interface becomes complicated.

These risks should be continuously monitored and reduced.

---

# User Success Metrics

Successful users typically:

* Capture information multiple times per day.
* Search instead of manually browsing.
* Return daily.
* Build a growing knowledge graph.
* Use AI conversations regularly.
* Enable reminders and notifications.
* Synchronize across devices.
* Upgrade after experiencing sustained value.
* Invite collaborators.

# Journey Completion

The journey does not end after installation.

Unlike many applications, YRecall becomes more valuable every day because every interaction strengthens the user's personal knowledge network.

Our long-term objective is for YRecall to evolve from:

A useful application

↓

A trusted productivity tool

↓

A daily AI assistant

↓

A lifelong digital companion.

# North Star

The ideal YRecall user journey ends when people no longer think about organizing information.

They simply live their lives, knowing YRecall will help them remember, understand, and act when the moment matters.
